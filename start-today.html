START TODAY

YOUR JOURNEY IS WAITING JUST FOR YOU

There is a saying about waiting until tomorrow. It never comes. For each new day that dawns a new tomorrow is born. Don't wait for that new year or tomorrow to start changing your life. Take the first simple step in your journey with a small action. It isn't so hard as it may seem. Watch a video, start a journal or pick a book that you will start to read each morning.

TAKE CONTROL

RESOURCES YOU CAN START WORKING WITH TODAY

SELF AUTHORING

The Self-Authoring Suite of online writing programs help you explore your past, present and future. People who write about themselves are happier, less anxious and healthier. They become more productive, persistent and engaged in life. This is because thinking about where you came from, who you are and where you are going helps you chart a simpler and more rewarding path through life.

TONY ROBBINS READING

Tony Robbins is an entrepreneur, philanthropist, author, and life/business coach. For over 4 decades, more than 50 million people have enjoyed his personal development events. He has written 6 books on personal development, read by over 4 million people. Tony maintains a curated reading list, these books are guaranteed to change your outlook on life.

12 RULES FOR LIFE

In this book, he provides twelve profound and practical principles for how to live a meaningful life, from setting your house in order before criticising others to comparing yourself to who you were yesterday, not someone else today. Happiness is a pointless goal, he shows us. Instead we must search for meaning, not for its own sake, but as a defence against the suffering that is intrinsic to our existence.

SELF JOURNAL

This 13-week goal planner, backed by science and success psychology, is designed to optimize your day, tackle your goals, and live a more fulfilled life. The Self Journal will help you set, plan and track progress towards your biggest goals and be more productive, overcome decision fatigue and focus on what matters most, prioritize your workload, build good habits and make every day count.
